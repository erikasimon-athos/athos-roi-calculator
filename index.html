How to find search sessions in GA4
  1. Log in to Google Analytics and select the GA4 property.
  2. In the left nav, click the Reports tab.
  3. Top-right, set the date range to Last 12 months and click Apply.
  4. Click the search bar at the top of the screen.
  5. Enter: Sessions for event name "view_search_results" in last 12 months
  6. Click the first result - a sidebar shows the session count.
  7. Enter that number in the Search sessions field above.
